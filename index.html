About

Welcome to my bio! I am Jun Ikeda, and this is the place where I brag on myself, exaggerate my accomplishments, and act goody-goody.

I am currently in the undergraduate program at California Institute of Technology (Pasadena CA), planning to major in Computer Science.

I have broad interests in human rights related problems and the application of technology for the social good, but specifically in EdTech. I'm going to join TechReach at Caltech and work together with nonprofit organizations to explore the potential impacts of technology.

I'm a Yanai Scholar'6, and receiving financial support to afford college expenses from Yanai Tadashi Foundation.

I support the philosophy of Peter Benenson and Amnesty International, democratic activists in Hong Kong and Ito Shiori.

By the way, my given name is Jun, but I wasn't born in June though I wish I were. (The moment of birth is the very first and biggest mistake in my life.)
