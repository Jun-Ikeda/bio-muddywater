About

Welcome to my bio! I am Jun Ikeda, and this is the place where I brag on myself, exerggerate my accomplishments, and act goody-goody.

I am currently in the undergraduate program at California Institute of Technology (Pasadena CA), planning to major in Computer Science.

I have broad interests in human rights related problems and the application of CS for the social good, but specifically in EdTech. I'm going to join TechReach at Caltech and work together with nonprofit organizations to explore the potential impacts of technology. I also like math btw.

I'm a Yanai Scholar'6, and receiving kind financial supports to afford college expenses from Yanai Tadashi Foundation.
